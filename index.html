What is

DDSS Ecommerz

DDSS Ecommerz is a dynamic and flexible single-vendor eCommerce platform, crafted to empower businesses with a comprehensive online presence. The platform integrates an easy-to-use admin panel, giving vendors full control over their storefront and operations. Customers enjoy a smooth and secure shopping experience thanks to the inclusion of multiple payment gateways, ensuring convenience and accessibility for diverse audiences. Additionally, the platform supports multiple SMS gateways for effective communication and notifications.

A standout feature of DDSS Ecommerz is its custom language functionality, allowing businesses to localize their offerings and connect with a global audience. Designed for scalability, efficiency, and user-friendliness, DDSS Ecommerz is the perfect solution for businesses looking to succeed in the competitive world of eCommerce.

Multiple Payment Gateways

DDSS Ecommerz offers seamless and secure transactions through a wide range of payment gateways, ensuring convenience for both vendors and customers. We support six popular payment methods, including PayPal, SSLCOMMERZ, bKash, Nagad, MyFatoorah, and Stripe, making it easy to process payments locally and internationally.

Our platform is continuously expanding, with even more payment gateway integrations coming soon, ensuring flexibility and choice for your customers. Whether you're selling products locally or globally, DDSS Ecommerz has you covered with trusted payment solutions.

Stunning Admin Pannel Presentation

The DDSS Ecommerz admin panel is designed to give you full control over your online store with ease. Fully responsive and user-friendly, the admin panel lets you manage every aspect of your business—from product listings and order processing to customer management and analytics—right from one centralized dashboard.

Whether you're on a desktop, tablet, or mobile device, the admin panel adapts seamlessly, ensuring you can efficiently run your store from anywhere, at any time. Experience intuitive navigation, real-time insights, and powerful tools to optimize your store’s performance effortlessly.

Order tracking System

With DDSS Ecommerz, customers can easily track their orders using a unique order code. Our order tracking system provides real-time updates, allowing users to monitor the progress of their purchases from the moment an order is placed until it arrives at their doorstep. From processing to shipping and delivery, customers can stay informed about every step of the journey, ensuring transparency and peace of mind throughout the entire process.

Frontend Technology

The frontend of our eCommerce website is built using HTML5 to provide a semantic and structured foundation, combined with CSS and Tailwind CSS for efficient, responsive design. JavaScript (JS) adds dynamic interactivity to the site, enabling real-time updates, form validation, and interactive elements. We also integrate jQuery to simplify DOM manipulation, event handling, and AJAX, enhancing the site's performance and user experience.

Backend Technology

Our backend is powered by Laravel, a PHP framework known for its elegant syntax and modular design, offering built-in authentication, routing, and security. MySQL serves as the database, managing product, customer, and transaction data efficiently. Eloquent ORM, Laravel’s ORM, simplifies database interactions, ensuring smooth and efficient data management. Together, these technologies deliver a secure, scalable, and high-performance backend for our eCommerce platform.

Responsive Design

DDSS Ecommerz delivers a seamless experience across all devices. Whether on desktop, tablet, or smartphone, the platform adapts effortlessly, providing smooth navigation and a consistent look for both customers and admins. Your store is always accessible, no matter the device.
